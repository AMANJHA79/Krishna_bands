About Us

Founded in 1998, Pandit Ji Wale Krishna Band has been adding grandeur and rhythm to weddings across Uttarakhand for over 25 years. With a deep-rooted passion for traditional music and a commitment to excellence, we have become a trusted name in wedding bands, ghori, baggi, dhol, and live musical performances. From majestic ghori and baggi processions to electrifying dhol beats and brass bands, we ensure that every baraat is a royal and unforgettable experience. Our team of skilled musicians, vibrant performers, and professional event planners work together to make your wedding day truly special.
